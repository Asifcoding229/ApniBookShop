Complete and Comprehensive Details About the PMA Initial Test

Basic knowledge of PMA test

The PMA Long Course in Pakistan Army is for regular commission officers of combat and combat support arms & services. The duration of PMA Long course is two years. After the 2-year training period, cadets pass out as 2nd Lieutenants.

How to Apply for PMA Long Course?

For Army officer selection through PMA, you need to go step by step:

  • Ensure you meet the PMA eligibility criteria.
  • Sign up for the PMA Initial Test and find out the syllabus.
  • Prepare smartly and score well to get selected for the later stages.

Are You Eligible for the PMA Initial Test?

The following requirements have to be fulfilled to join the Pak Army:

Criteria Details
Nationality Citizens of Pakistan and domicile holders of Azad Kashmir/Gilgit-Baltistan.
Gender Male
Marital Status Unmarried (Married serving personnel over 20 years of age are eligible).
Age
  • Intermediate: 17-22 Years
  • Graduates (2 years): 17-23 Years
  • Graduates (4 years): 17-24 Years
  • Serving Army Soldiers: 17-25 Years
Height Minimum 5’4” (162.5 cm)

PMA Initial Test Syllabus

1) Intelligence Test:

  • Verbal Intelligence Test (85-100 Questions)
  • Non-Verbal Intelligence Test (85-100 Questions)

2) Academic Test: (40 Questions)

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Pakistan Studies
  • Islamiat
  • General Knowledge

Preparation Tips

To score well, follow these tips:

  • Solve maximum questions for PMA MCQ exam preparation.
  • Attempt as many PMA practice questions as possible.
  • Use smart resources like Tabir or Dogar.pk for effective preparation.
